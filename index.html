Rodolfo Anibal Lobo

ABOUT ME

I am a Ph.D in applied mathematics from the University of Campinas, Brazil. My research interests include computational intelligence and biomathematics. I am currently developing hypercomplex-valued neural network models. The main applications for these models are associative memories for pattern reconstruction and classification.

Currently, I am working at the IT and software development company Globant as a Data Scientist. I have experienced with NLP and audio applications using machine learning and deep learning models.
